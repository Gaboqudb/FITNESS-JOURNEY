A minimal fitness roadmap combining beginner-friendly gym sessions and practical nutrition habits so you can build strength, feel energized, and stay consistent.

Weekly Gym Plan

  • Monday: Push day — bench press, shoulder press, tricep dips.
  • Wednesday: Pull day — rows, lat pulldowns, bicep curls.
  • Friday: Legs + core — squats, RDLs, lunges, planks.
  • Saturday: Optional cardio — 25 min brisk incline walk.

Nutrition Foundations

  • Prioritize protein at every meal (eggs, chicken, fish, tofu, Greek yogurt).
  • Build plates with 50% vegetables, 25% lean protein, 25% quality carbs.
  • Hydrate consistently: target 2–3 liters of water daily.
  • Use simple prep: cook once, eat twice for easier adherence.

Technique & Progress

  • Warm up for 8–10 minutes with light cardio and mobility.
  • Choose weights that leave 1–2 reps in reserve on each set.
  • Increase one variable weekly: reps, weight, or quality of form.
  • Sleep 7–9 hours to recover and support muscle growth.
